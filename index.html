Capital deployed across three categories where we have genuine edge — early-stage startups where we can add operational value, curated collectibles with strong fundamentals, and endurance sports as both an asset class and a personal conviction.

Angel Investments

Early-stage checks in B2C and fintech startups where we can contribute more than capital — strategic guidance, product intuition, growth frameworks, and network access earned across nine years of building and advising.

  • B2C, fintech, and consumer software
  • Founder-operator support alongside capital

A curated approach to tangible assets with strong cultural cachet and long-term appreciation potential. We apply the same analytical rigor to collectibles markets as we do to business strategy — identifying undervalued assets with durable fundamentals.

  • Athlete-founder partnerships
